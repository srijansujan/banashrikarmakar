Banashri lives with her parents and a younger sister in a one-room thatched hut, in the village of Bhadiswar near Murarai in Birbhum. The sole breadwinner in the family is her father who works in a local cycle repairing shop. Poverty and abject conditions notwithstanding, Banashri went on to score 92% in her Higher Secondary with generous help from some of her school teachers. She joined Kabi Nazrul College in 2016 to pursue a bachelor’s in Bengali. Srijan Sujan has promised to assist her in her endeavours. Recently Banashri has quit her B.A studies to enrol in the GNM training course. She is currently in the first year of training at the LDV Hospital in Kolkata.
